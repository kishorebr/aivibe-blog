This latest addition lets it dive deep into your work conversations to search for information that never makes it into formal documents -- from specific project details to summaries of your day's messages.Google offered a few example scenarios. You might ask something like, "Who's the marketing lead for Project Clover?," "What's the latest deadline mentioned for Project X?" or "Summarize my unread chat messages from today." Also: You can turn off Gemini in Gmail, Photos, Chrome, and more - here's how Tools like Gemini can be useful at work, but they're limited to the information you give them when it comes to personal help.
