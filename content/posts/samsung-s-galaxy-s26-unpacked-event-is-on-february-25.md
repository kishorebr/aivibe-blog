<p>After kicking off CES 2026 with its <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/home/smart-home/samsung-unveiled-ai-powered-products-at-ces-2026-everything-announced-from-the-years-first-press-conference-230059247.html" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1">&quot;First Look&quot; event</a>, Samsung is ready to announce the first of what should be several new Galaxy smartphones this year. The company <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://news.samsung.com/us/samsung-galaxy-unpacked-february-2026-next-ai-phone-makes-life-easier/" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">is officially hosting</a> a Galaxy Unpacked event on February 25 at 1PM ET, where it&#39;ll introduce the Galaxy S26 series and updates to Galaxy AI.</p><p>Leaks that have trickled out ahead of the event suggest that the Galaxy S26, S26+ and S26 Ultra will feature a new <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/mobile/smartphones/qualcomms-new-flagship-mobile-platform-is-the-snapdragon-8-elite-gen-5-060427445.html" data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1">Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5</a> chip, and could come with <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://x.com/UniverseIce/status/1994663953180627176?s=20" data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1">more RAM and storage</a>. Only the <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.androidheadlines.com/2026/01/galaxy-s26-ultra-will-look-more-premium-than-the-s25-ultra.html" data-i13n="cpos:5;pos:1">Galaxy S26 Ultra</a> is expected to include major hardware changes, though, with an updated camera system, and possibly proper support for Qi2 charging. Alongside new smartphones, Samsung is also expected to introduce the <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.sammobile.com/news/galaxy-buds-4-pro-hints-surface-alongside-a-bunch-of-other-unreleased-products/" data-i13n="cpos:6;pos:1">Galaxy Buds 4 and 4 Pro</a>, which will reportedly feature a new design, support for head gestures and an Ultra Wideband chip so they&#39;re easier to find using Google Find Hub.</p><p>As in previous years, Samsung has an optional deal for anyone who wants to lock in a discount before the company&#39;s new smartphones and accessories are announced.
