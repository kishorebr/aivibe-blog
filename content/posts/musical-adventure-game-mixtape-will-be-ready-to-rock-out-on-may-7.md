<div><div style="left:0;width:100%;height:0;position:relative;padding-bottom:56.25%;"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ITXbyF-zYiM?rel=0" style="top:0;left:0;width:100%;height:100%;position:absolute;border:0;" allowfullscreen scrolling="no" data-embed-domain="youtube.com" data-provider-name="youtube"></iframe></div></div><p>Time to dust off your boomboxes and your walkmans: musical coming-of-age game <em>Mixtape</em> is due out this spring. At its <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/mixtape-brings-a-killer-80s-soundtrack-to-xbox-and-pc-in-2025-181740393.html" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1">reveal</a>, this next title from game developer Beethoven and Dinosaur was targeting a 2025 release, but as happens to the best laid plans, the game&#39;s arrival got pushed back. Today&#39;s trailer announced that the new launch date for the narrative- and music-focused adventure game is May 7, 2026. Despite the new date, <em>Mixtape</em> will still be coming to the usual array of current gaming platforms: Steam and the Epic Games Store, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S and the Nintendo Switch 2.&nbsp;</p><p>Johnny Galvatron, game director for the project, <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/the-creators-of-mixtape-want-to-make-a-great-hangout-video-game-140026928.html" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">spoke with Engadget</a> recently about the process of conveying that familiar feeling of restless teenage ennui in this medium. &quot;Idleness is hard to explore as a video game, and one of the interesting things about being a teenager is you just hang out a lot, and sometimes it just sucks,&quot; Galvatron said. &quot;So I love that we made a game that shows that idleness.”</p><p>As the name suggests, the soundtrack is core to the experience of this game about three friends at the end of high school. Expect to hear tracks by iconic punk and alternative artists such as DEVO, Joy Division, Siouxsie and the Banshees, Smashing Pumpkins and Iggy Pop.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/gaming/musical-adventure-game-mixtape-will-be-ready-to-rock-out-on-may-7-200500458.html?src=rss
