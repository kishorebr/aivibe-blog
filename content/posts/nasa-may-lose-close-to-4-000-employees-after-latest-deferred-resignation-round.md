<p>The second round of deferred resignations for NASA staff closed on Friday, and the agency says roughly 3,000 employees applied to leave, according to <a data-i13n="elm:context_link;elmt:doNotAffiliate;cpos:1;pos:1" class="no-affiliate-link" href="https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-07-26/nasa-says-thousands-of-employees-set-to-resign-from-space-agency"><em><ins>Bloomberg</ins></em></a>. The Trump administration first <a data-i13n="elm:context_link;elmt:doNotAffiliate;cpos:2;pos:1" class="no-affiliate-link" href="https://apnews.com/article/doge-firings-layoffs-federal-government-workers-musk-d33cdd7872d64d2bdd8fe70c28652654"><ins>offered the deferred resignation program</ins></a> as a buyout to government workers in January as it gutted the federal workforce under the guidance of <a data-i13n="elm:context_link;elmt:doNotAffiliate;cpos:3;pos:1" class="no-affiliate-link" href="https://www.engadget.com/big-tech/doge-is-reportedly-pushing-an-ai-tool-that-would-put-half-of-all-federal-regulations-on-a-delete-list-212053871.html"><ins>DOGE</ins></a> — <a data-i13n="elm:context_link;elmt:doNotAffiliate;cpos:4;pos:1" class="no-affiliate-link" href="https://www.engadget.com/big-tech/elon-musk-leaves-doge-amid-tanking-tesla-sales-153859407.html"><ins>then led by Elon Musk</ins></a> — asking employees to resign while still receiving benefits and pay for a period of time. In the earlier round, 870 NASA employees reportedly opted to leave. The space agency opened a second round in June, with a July 25 deadline.</p>
<p>The latest batch of applications brings the total to nearly 4,000 employees, or roughly 20 percent of NASA&#39;s workforce, according to a statement provided to <em>Bloomberg</em>. It comes after <a data-i13n="cpos:5;pos:1" href="https://www.politico.com/news/2025/07/09/nasa-staff-departures-00444674"><em><ins>Politico</ins></em></a> reported earlier this month that over 2,000 senior NASA staff members <a data-i13n="cpos:6;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/science/space/nasa-will-lose-over-2000-senior-staff-due-to-proposed-trump-budget-cuts-210109229.html"><ins>have agreed to leave</ins></a>.</p>
<span id="end-legacy-contents"></span><p>NASA is grappling with proposed <a data-i13n="cpos:7;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/science/space/trumps-defunding-of-nasa-would-be-catastrophic-153053020.html"><ins>budget cuts that could crush the agency&#39;s science programs</ins></a> and result in the loss of thousands of jobs.
