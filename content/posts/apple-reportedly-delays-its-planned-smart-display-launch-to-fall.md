<p>Mark Gurman at <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-09/apple-postpones-smart-home-display-launch-as-it-waits-for-new-ai-and-siri?srnd=undefined&amp;embedded-checkout=true" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1"><em>Bloomberg</em></a> is back with the latest rumors about what&#39;s afoot with Apple&#39;s future plans, and how its ongoing difficulties with artificial intelligence seem to be creating further delays for its next wave of product launches. His sources say that Apple is expected to postpone the debut of its smart home display until later in 2026, likely September when it often introduces new gadgets. Although the hardware has reportedly been finished for months, this delay is being credited to the company&#39;s AI-centric overhaul of Siri still not being complete.</p><p>The device, internally known as J490, has been one of Apple&#39;s many poorly-kept secrets. Rumors about a HomePod smart speaker coupled with a screen first emerged <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/apple-smart-home-devices-report-155130684.html" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">back in 2022</a> and have <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/home/smart-home/apples-rumored-smart-display-may-arrive-in-2025-running-new-homeos-212401853.html" data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1">resurfaced</a> from <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/home/smart-home/apples-smart-home-hub-could-reportedly-make-its-debut-later-this-year-161702487.html" data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1">time</a> to <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/home/apples-long-rumored-smart-display-will-reportedly-cost-350-165801748.html" data-i13n="cpos:5;pos:1">time</a> in the interim, often with promises that the device&#39;s arrival was imminent. The latest claims anticipated that the official announcement was coming this spring, possibly as soon as this month. However, appears to Apple once again be hamstrung by an AI strategy that has left it scrambling to catch up to other industry leaders.</p><p>Apple has been working to incorporate more AI capabilities into Siri for <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/ai/apple-is-delaying-its-smarter-more-personal-siri-183513424.html" data-i13n="cpos:6;pos:1">more than a year</a> as part of its Apple Intelligence package. Gurman reports that the new timeline from Apple aims to have the revamp completed for the launch of the iPhone 18 Pro, which is also expected for September. Apple may unveil this long-awaited <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/ai/apple-is-reportedly-overhauling-siri-to-be-an-ai-chatbot-205303818.html" data-i13n="cpos:7;pos:1">Siri-as-chatbot</a> during its WWDC keynote in the summer before it shows up in any devices.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/home/smart-home/apple-reportedly-delays-its-planned-smart-display-launch-to-fall-194539082.html?src=rss
