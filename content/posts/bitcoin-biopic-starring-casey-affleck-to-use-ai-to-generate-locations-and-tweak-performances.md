The film was <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://variety.com/2025/film/news/doug-liman-casey-affleck-pete-davidson-bitcoin-killing-satoshi-1236501408/" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">announced in 2025</a> as being directed by Doug Liman (<em>The Bourne Identity</em>, <em>The Edge of Tomorrow</em>) and starring Casey Affleck and Pete Davidson in undisclosed roles, but its connection to overhyped technology was previously understood to begin and end with cryptocurrency.</p><p>According to a UK casting notice viewed by <em>Variety</em>, the producers of <em>Killing Satoshi</em> reserve the right to &quot;change, add to, take from, translate, reformat or reprocess‚Äù actors&#39; performances, using &quot;generative artificial intelligence (GAI) and/or machine learning technologies.&quot; No digital replicas will be created of performers, but it sounds like plenty of other AI-driven tweaks are on the table.
