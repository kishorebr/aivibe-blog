<p class="has-text-align-none">Sen. Ed Markey (D-MA) is pressing OpenAI about its move to bring ads to ChatGPT, and is asking several other companies whether they have similar plans. In <a href="https://www.markey.senate.gov/imo/media/doc/ai_chatbot_ad_letter.pdf">letters to the CEOs of OpenAI</a>, Anthropic, Google, Meta, Microsoft, Snap, and xAI, Markey writes that embedding ads into AI chatbots "raises significant concerns for consumer protection, privacy, and the safety of young users."</p>
