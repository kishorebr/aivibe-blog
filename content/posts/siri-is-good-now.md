<p class="has-text-align-none">You'd be forgiven for thinking this day would never come. But the wildest thing just happened: Apple <a href="https://www.theverge.com/tech/942416/apple-siri-ai-update-wwdc">put out a new version of Siri</a>, and it actually <a href="https://www.theverge.com/tech/947432/siri-ai-apple-intelligence-ios-27-wwdc">seems to be pretty good</a>.</p>
<p class="has-text-align-none">On <a href="https://pod.link/vergecast">this episode of <em>The Vergecast</em></a>, David and Nilay talk about their early experiences with Siri AI, and what it means for users, and the rest of the AI industry, for the iPhone's built-in assistant to be good enough at most things. There's very little about Siri AI that feels bleeding edge or brand new, but …</p>
