This sequel to the hugely popular 2019 roguelike deckbuilder hits early access on Steam beginning March 5, 2026. Along with releasing the teaser trailer above, developer Mega Crit shared some <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/2868840/Slay_the_Spire_2/" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1">details</a> about its goals for this phase ahead of the game&#39;s official release.</p><p>&quot;<em>Slay the Spire</em> requires a lot of player feedback so we can balance content, add quality of life features, and make sure the game runs without issues,&quot; the developers explained. &quot;Early Access is also a chance for us to test experimental features, try exotic designs, identify niche problems, and helps us make sure the game is headed in the right direction.&quot; <em>Slay the Spire 2</em> is expected to be in early access for a year or two, or more generally &quot;until the game feels great.&quot;</p><p>Mega Crit has also revealed that it will be introducing a new co-op mode where up to four people can team up. This gameplay option will feature some unique cards just for multiplayer as well as some team-wide synergies.&nbsp;</p><p>Some of the characters from <em>Slay the Spire</em> will be returning for the sequel alongside new ones. For anyone who hasn&#39;t yet experienced the original game, <em>Slay the Spire</em> is <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/slay-the-spire-ios-150017220.html" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">available</a> on iOS, including as <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/stardew-valley-slay-the-spire-and-ridiculous-fishing-are-coming-to-apple-arcade-in-july-153459825.html" data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1">part of</a> Apple Arcade.
