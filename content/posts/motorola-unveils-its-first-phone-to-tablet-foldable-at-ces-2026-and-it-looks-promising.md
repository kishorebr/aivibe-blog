You may be wondering, "Doesn't Motorola already have that?" Well, not exactly. Until now, the modern Razr lineup has consisted entirely of clamshell-style devices. Opening the device reveals a large 8.1-inch 2K LTPO inner display. To put that into perspective, this is a larger smartphone than Google's Pixel 10 Pro Fold, which has a 6.4-inch outer screen and an 8-inch inner display.
