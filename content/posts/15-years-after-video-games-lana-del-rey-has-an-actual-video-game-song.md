<div><div style="left:0;width:100%;height:0;position:relative;padding-bottom:56.25%;"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/7cqG60pjDS0?rel=0" style="top:0;left:0;width:100%;height:100%;position:absolute;border:0;" allowfullscreen scrolling="no" data-embed-domain="youtube.com" data-provider-name="youtube"></iframe></div></div><p>The James Bond franchise has a long history of getting pop stars to record its theme songs (perhaps most memorably with <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b07Z_qfchFk" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1"><em>Live and Let Die</em></a>), and it looks like that tradition will now extend to video game adaptations about the fictional spy. <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://ioi.dk/?panel=007firstlightgame/news/2026/007-first-light-unveils-title-song-by-lana-del-rey-and-david-arnold" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">IO Interactive has announced</a> that Lana Del Rey co-wrote and performed the theme for <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/io-interactives-007-first-light-reimagines-james-bond-as-a-young-and-reckless-spy-180000758.html" data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1"><em>007 First Light</em></a>, the developer&#39;s playable James Bond origin story. </p><p>&quot;First Light&quot; is written and performed by Lana Del Rey and composer David Arnold, and like <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7cqG60pjDS0" data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1">the moody and abstract opening credits</a> released alongside the song, could vaguely gesture at the themes of the game. IO Interactive has previously said that its game focuses on a young, inexperienced and more reckless Bond, before he developed his trademark cool. The developer is also integrating the stealth mechanics it perfected in <em>Hitman </em>into the upcoming game.</p><p>Del Rey&#39;s personal gaming experience may begin and end with her hit &quot;Video Games,&quot; which <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.bbc.com/news/entertainment-arts-16729651" data-i13n="cpos:5;pos:1">was apparently written</a> about a former boyfriend&#39;s love of <em>World of Warcraft</em>, but the artist does know how to write a song with Bond in mind. <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cv22mmnjrnyo" data-i13n="cpos:6;pos:1">Lana Del Rey shared</a> in 2024 that her song <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pkIYYpAjsjo&amp;list=RDpkIYYpAjsjo&amp;start_radio=1" data-i13n="cpos:7;pos:1">&quot;24&quot;</a> from the album <em>Honeymoon</em> was originally written for 2017&#39;s <em>Spectre</em>, one of several songs that were cast aside in favor of Sam Smith&#39;s <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8jzDnsjYv9A&amp;list=RD8jzDnsjYv9A&amp;start_radio=1" data-i13n="cpos:8;pos:1">&quot;Writing&#39;s on the Wall.&quot;</a></p><p><em>007 First Light</em> is coming to Xbox Series X/S, PlayStation 5 and PC on May 27, 2026. A Nintendo Switch 2 version of the game is now <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/nintendo/the-nintendo-switch-2-version-of-007-first-light-is-delayed-until-later-this-summer-151524487.html" data-i13n="cpos:9;pos:1">coming out this summer</a>.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/gaming/15-years-after-video-games-lana-del-rey-has-an-actual-video-game-song-221925735.html?src=rss
