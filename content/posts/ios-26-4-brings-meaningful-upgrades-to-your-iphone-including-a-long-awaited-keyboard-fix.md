Apple Music First up are some clever additions to the Apple Music app. With the new Playlist Playground, you can use AI to create the type of playlist you want. For example, tell the app that you want it to build a playlist of jazz tunes from the 1950s, and it should generate one based on your music library. The new Concerts feature points you to nearby live shows by artists who appear in your library. You'll also receive recommendations for new artists and shows based on your favorites.
