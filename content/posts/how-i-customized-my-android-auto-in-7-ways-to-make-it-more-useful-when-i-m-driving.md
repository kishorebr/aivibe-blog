Customize your app launcherTired of seeing apps you never use in Android Auto? You can delete ones you don't want and prioritize the ones you do, so you don't have to scroll every time to find them. Head to Settings, search for Android Auto, and look for Customize Launcher. You'll have the option to check or uncheck the apps you want to appear, or set a custom order rather than alphabetical order. 2.
