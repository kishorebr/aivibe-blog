<p>Spring has certainly sprung here at Engadget. Well, it has in terms of <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/reviews/" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1">reviews</a>, at least. We’ve put over a dozen devices through their paces since my last roundup, which gives you a lot to catch up on over the weekend. Read on for the rundown of all the reviews you might’ve missed. </p>
<h2 id="jump-link-asus-zenbook-a16">ASUS ZenBook A16</h2>
<p>
 <core-commerce data-type="product-list" id="65a8196d-b8ee-4a84-a747-b8f28fb314ed" data-original-url="https://urldefense.com/v3/__https://bestbuy.com/product/asus-zenbook-a16-16-3k-oled-laptop-copilot-pc-snapdragon-x2-elite-extreme-48gb-ram-1tb-ssd-zabriskie-beige/JJGHGSCXZV/sku/6671011__;!!Op6eflyXZCqGR5I!BLDGzrhpnN06Ld9b0Ao0RBCLa0PJkVB3Mi0rzPhYsPuNYOcsjMhLa_zS7NfB7PPK4ZsGBhHW-A5-kd9zSwulYnQ$"></core-commerce></p>
<p>ASUS’ ZenBook A14 didn’t live up to our expectations last year, but now the company is back with a 16-inch machine and a shot at redemption: <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/computing/laptops/asus-zenbook-a16-review-a-surprisingly-light-and-powerful-16-inch-ultraportable-140000914.html" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">the A16</a>. “Compatibility issues aside, the ZenBook A16 delivers just about everything I want in an ultraportable,” senior reporter Devindra Hardawar said. “It’s got a gorgeous OLED screen and all of the ports you need. Qualcomm’s Snapdragon X2 Elite chips also give it a much-needed power boost. And best of all, it's one of the lightest and sleekest 16-inch Windows laptops I've come across.”</p>
<h2 id="jump-link-apple-airpods-max-2">Apple AirPods Max 2</h2>
<p>
 <core-commerce data-type="product-list" id="d8d4a5ee-fe0b-481f-a5ac-e6afafe2c5b6" data-original-url="https://www.apple.com/shop/buy-airpods/airpods-max-2/starlight"></core-commerce></p>
<p>Until this year, Apple’s only updates to the AirPods Max were new colors and a USB-C port. The company finally gave its <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/audio/headphones/apple-airpods-max-2-review-better-late-than-never-130000982.html" data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1">pricey over-ear headphones</a> the powerful H2 chip, delivering a host of handy features from the AirPods Pro. “The H2 chip brings Apple’s over-ear headphones on par with the rest of the AirPods lineup, namely the AirPods Pro 3,” I said. “And since I don’t expect Apple to announce new earbuds this year, that parity should remain for a while.”</p>
<h2 id="jump-link-sonos-play">Sonos Play</h2>
<p>
 <core-commerce data-type="product-list" id="4bd8c4d6-2ffd-4ca1-9c56-1e3ccc4e7a13" data-original-url="https://www.sonos.com/en-us/shop/sonos-play"></core-commerce></p>
<p>Sonos badly needed a win. Thankfully, the company regained some of its mojo with <a target="_blank" rel="" class="link" href="https://cm-ui.yahoo.com/story/d3973941-767b-461c-8982-bf83da0f8e3c" data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1">a new portable speaker</a> that offers the best of Bluetooth and Wi-Fi in the same device. “The latest Sonos speaker offers impressive sound quality, flexibility and portability, and it’s the kind of product that can help Sonos rebuild its reputation after its recent difficulties,” deputy editor Nathan Ingraham said.</p>
<h2 id="jump-link-lg-sound-suite">LG Sound Suite</h2>
<p>
 <core-commerce data-type="product-list" id="8f2cefa7-2799-4b2d-8e52-8d51cb0caf73" data-original-url="https://www.amazon.com/stores/page/6565552A-B15A-4557-88C1-54745F2B72CF"></core-commerce></p>
<p>After an impressive CES debut, <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/audio/speakers/lg-sound-suite-review-dolby-atmos-flexconnect-in-a-powerful-package-160000544.html?guccounter=1&amp;guce_referrer=aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuZW5nYWRnZXQuY29tLw&amp;guce_referrer_sig=AQAAAEkBseOfNiYojgJTw2w5sRGMJuduBxhEdBSHkoeo5LqF9fwLllouVcENQjh_DNoBpdO-ojcqS-mL1YULbQQIdOcc2x4Bs6JreTo68rwuex-NrH29tg4uSk6w3uoUbqHTs2gN5trjauUgzdJeuVbB_Amic_p4wGGR3MYmVhaG4fTY" data-i13n="cpos:5;pos:1">LG’s Sound Suite</a> was my most anticipated review of the year. Despite impressive sound quality and Dolby Atmos FlexConnect, there are still some kinks to work out in both the setup and general use. “There’s no denying that LG has created a powerful and immersive living room experience with its Sound Suite lineup,” I said. “While I did experience some setup and software issues, those are things LG can iron out over time — Sound Suite is still brand new, after all.”</p>
