<p>Waymo has <a data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1" href="https://waymo.com/blog/"><ins>announced</ins></a> the launch of its robotaxi services in three new cities. San Diego, Las Vegas and Detroit will play home to the driverless cars as the company continues its <a data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/tag/waymo/"><ins>aggressive expansion</ins></a>.</p>
<p>In a series of <a data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1" href="https://waymo.com/blog/"><ins>blog posts</ins></a>, the Alphabet subsidiary said Detroit residents can expect to &quot;soon&quot; see Waymo vehicles on the streets, mapping out the service areas before launching to the public. Timing for San Diego was more vague, with &quot;plans to begin serving the city next year.&quot;</p>
<span id="end-legacy-contents"></span><p>Waymo&#39;s service area in Las Vegas will include the Strip, with plans to expand to the airport &quot;eventually.&quot; The company currently operates in Los Angeles, Phoenix, San Francisco, Atlanta and Austin. It also recently announced expansion to <a data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/transportation/waymo-announces-its-expanding-to-miami-204504533.html"><ins>Miami</ins></a> and <a data-i13n="cpos:5;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/transportation/waymo-aims-to-offer-paid-robotaxi-rides-in-washington-dc-next-year-161523916.html"><ins>Washington, DC</ins></a>.</p>
<p>Waymo also has its sights set on international expansion with a planned <a data-i13n="cpos:6;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/transportation/evs/waymo-plans-to-launch-fully-autonomous-taxi-service-in-london-next-year-142453250.html"><ins>London launch</ins></a> next spring. This comes as the UK&#39;s <a data-i13n="cpos:7;pos:1" href="https://www.gov.uk/government/news/self-driving-vehicles-set-to-be-on-roads-by-2026-as-automated-vehicles-act-becomes-law"><ins>Automated Vehicles Act of 2024</ins></a> begins to take effect, allowing autonomous vehicles to operate in the country for the first time. The company also recently announced it would be partnering with <a data-i13n="cpos:8;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/transportation/waymo-will-also-drive-for-doordash-in-phoenix-213535127.html"><ins>DoorDash</ins></a> to conduct food deliveries in Phoenix.</p>
<p>The autonomous taxi market has been heating up lately with Tesla&#39;s Robotaxi <a data-i13n="cpos:9;pos:1" href="https://www.teslarati.com/tesla-ramp-500-robotaxis-austin-1000-bay-area-end-of-2025-musk/"><ins>expanding</ins></a> in Austin and San Francisco. Uber and Lucid will also be launching an autonomous taxi partnership in the Bay Area next year using the automaker&#39;s <a data-i13n="cpos:10;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/transportation/evs/the-lucid-gravity-is-the-coolest-three-row-ev-on-the-market-120024089.html"><ins>Lucid Gravity SUV</ins></a>.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/transportation/waymo-is-launching-in-three-new-cities-next-year-182515034.html?src=rss
