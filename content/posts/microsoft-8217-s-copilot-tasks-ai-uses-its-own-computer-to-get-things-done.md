<p class="has-text-align-none">Microsoft is previewing a new AI system, Copilot Tasks, that it says is designed to take care of busywork for you in the background, the <a href="https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-copilot/blog/2026/02/26/copilot-tasks-from-answers-to-actions/">company announced on Thursday</a>. The feature takes the load off your device using its own cloud-based computer and browser, allowing it to handle a variety of jobs ranging from scheduling appointments to generating study plans while you do something else.</p>
<p class="has-text-align-none">As noted by Microsoft, you can describe what you need to Copilot Tasks using natural language, and assign Copilot Tasks to complete jobs on a recurring, scheduled, or one-time basis. Copilot Tasks will provide a report once its work is complete.</p>
