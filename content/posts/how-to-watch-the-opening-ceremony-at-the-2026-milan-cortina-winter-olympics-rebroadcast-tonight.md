<h2 id="jump-link-who-is-on-the-team-usa-hockey-teams">Who hosted the Winter Olympics Opening Ceremony?</h2> 
<p>Sportscaster Terry Gannon hosted coverage of the 2026 Winter Games Opening Ceremony. Former Olympic snowboarder Shaun White also made an appearance. NBC Olympics primetime host Mike Tirico also participated remotely from San Francisco, where he&#39;s pulling double duty covering the Olympics and prepping to call Super Bowl LX.&nbsp;</p> 
<h2 id="jump-link-who-is-performing-at-the-2026-winter-olympics-opening-ceremony">Who performed at the 2026 Winter Olympics Opening Ceremony?</h2> 
<p>Mariah Carey, iconic Italian tenor Andrea Bocelli, actress Sabrina Impacciatore (<em>The Paper, The White Lotus</em>) and pianist Lang Lang performed at the Opening Ceremony.</p> 
<h2 id="jump-link-where-is-the-2026-olympics-opening-ceremony-being-held">Where is the 2026 Olympics Opening Ceremony being held?</h2> 
<p>The 2026 Winter Olympics Opening Ceremony was held at Milan&#39;s San Siro Stadium, home to football clubs AC Milan and Inter Milan. The Opening Ceremony will actually be one of the final events held at San Siro Stadium, which is set to be demolished sometime after the Games end.</p>
