<p class="has-text-align-none">ChatGPT is <a href="https://www.theverge.com/news/837280/spotify-wrapped-2025-recap-annual-best-of-data">joining the flood of apps</a> offering yearly recaps for users. It's <a href="https://x.com/OpenAI/status/2003190103729144224?s=20">rolling out a "Year in Review" feature</a> that will show you a bunch of stats - like how many messages you sent to the chatbot in 2025 - as well as give you an AI-generated pixel art-style image that encompasses some of the topics you talked about this year. </p>
<p class="has-text-align-none">The image I received showed an aquarium beside a game cartridge, an Instant Pot, and a computer screen, reflecting some of the questions I had related to retro game consoles, cooking, and my fish tank setup.</p>
