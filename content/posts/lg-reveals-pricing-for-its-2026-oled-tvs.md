<p>Now for the news you&#39;ve been waiting with bated breath for: LG&#39;s 2026 TVs from CES finally have prices. (Well, some of them do, anyway.) Surprisingly, the evo G6 and C6 series OLED TVs aren&#39;t increasing in cost from <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/home/home-theater/lgs-oled-evo-tvs-for-2025-come-with-ai-and-a-165hz-refresh-rate-200037568.html" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1">last year&#39;s models</a>. But the bad news is, they’re still expensive as all get-out.</p><p>The flagship LG evo G6 series ranges in price from $2,499 to $24,999. (Cue spit take.) Fortunately, that five-figure price only applies to the 97-inch model, which nobody <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/tesla-shareholders-approve-elon-musks-1-trillion-compensation-package-233217531.html" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">this side of Elon Musk</a> needs. The entry-level price is for a 55-inch OLED. Moving up the ladder, the 65-inch one costs $3,399, the 77-inch model is $4,499 and an 83-incher will set you back $6,499.</p><p>The evo G6 line includes all the OLED upgrades from the <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/home/home-theater/lg-tvs-at-ces-2026-a-stunning-wallpaper-set-glorious-micro-rgb-colors-and-a-better-gallery-tv-033739600.html" data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1">head-turning LG Wallpaper TV</a>, for which LG <em>hasn&#39;t</em> yet announced pricing. You&#39;ll find the company&#39;s new &quot;Hyper Radiant OLED&quot; panel and optimizations to black and color levels in both lineups.</p><p>Meanwhile, the evo C6 line, which sits a notch below, ranges from $1,399 (42-inch) to $5,299 (83-inch). Rounding out the list is a 55-inch model for $1,999, a 65-inch one for $2,699 and a 77-inch model for $3,699. The C6 and G6 lines are powered by LG&#39;s Alpha 11 AI Processor Gen3. Both series support gamer-friendly features such as 4K at 165Hz with VRR, NVIDIA G-SYNC and AMD FreeSync Premium.</p><p>The evo G6 and C6 lines are available to order today from <a target="_blank" class="link rapid-with-clickid" href="https://shopping.yahoo.com/rdlw?merchantId=472a467b-5d76-4e49-b1e8-d1e77258dd8e&amp;siteId=us-engadget&amp;pageId=1p-autolink&amp;contentUuid=0f351b75-103e-4db3-af93-a685d6a637a4&amp;featureId=text-link&amp;merchantName=LG+Electronics&amp;linkText=LG%27s+website&amp;custData=eyJzb3VyY2VOYW1lIjoiV2ViLURlc2t0b3AtVmVyaXpvbiIsImxhbmRpbmdVcmwiOiJodHRwczovL3d3dy5sZy5jb20vdXMvb2xlZC10dnMiLCJjb250ZW50VXVpZCI6IjBmMzUxYjc1LTEwM2UtNGRiMy1hZjkzLWE2ODVkNmE2MzdhNCIsIm9yaWdpbmFsVXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubGcuY29tL3VzL29sZWQtdHZzIn0&amp;signature=AQAAAcZXaMtfgXE1JnKe9AObR71WCKyC4JfBOyPgXOWFMJMZ&amp;gcReferrer=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.lg.com%2Fus%2Foled-tvs" data-i13n="elm:affiliate_link;sellerN:LG Electronics;elmt:;cpos:4;pos:1" data-original-link="https://www.lg.com/us/oled-tvs">LG&#39;s website</a>.
