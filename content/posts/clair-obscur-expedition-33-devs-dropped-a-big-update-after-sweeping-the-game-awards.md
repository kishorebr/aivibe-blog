<p>Sandfall Interactive, the developer of <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/clair-obscur-expedition-33-preview-stunning-visuals-innovative-combat-prime-melodrama-140052194.html" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1"><em>Clair Obscur: Expedition 33</em></a>, knows how to ride the wave. Right after the visually stunning RPG finished dominating The Game Awards, the team <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.expedition33.com/post/thank-you-update-available-now-thank-you-for-an-amazing-year" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">dropped</a> a free &quot;Thank You&quot; content update for fans.</p><p>This is no minor patch. It adds a playable environment and new boss battles for late-game players. The &quot;Thank You&quot; update also adds new music tracks, a Photo Mode and new text and UI localizations. On top of that, you&#39;ll find quality-of-life tweaks, including improved performance on <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/best-handheld-gaming-system-140018863.html" data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1">handheld PCs</a>.</p><p><em>Clair Obscur: Expedition 33</em> <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/everything-announced-and-all-the-winners-at-the-game-awards-2025-044101761.html" data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1">won a record-breaking nine categories</a> at The Game Awards 2025. That includes — deep breath — Game of the Year, Best RPG, Best Art Direction, Best Game Direction, Best Narrative, Best Performance (Jennifer English), Best Score and Music, Best Debut Game and Best Indie Game. The previous record was <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/the-last-of-us-part-2-remastered-review-the-roguelike-no-return-mode-steals-the-show-150006382.html" data-i13n="cpos:5;pos:1"><em>The Last of Us Part 2</em></a>, which took home seven awards in 2020.</p><p>The game&#39;s free &quot;Thank You&quot; update is rolling out now.
