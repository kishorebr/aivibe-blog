    3 ways the new Steam Machine could be a huge win for Linux
     
    Valve has finally released information on the Steam Machine pricing and release timing. Here's how to get on the waitlist and why Linux users should rejoice.
      Written by 
            Jack Wallen, Contributing WriterContributing Writer  June 23, 2026 at 1:37 p.m. PT                           ValveFollow ZDNET: Add us as a preferred source on Google.ZDNET key takeawaysSteam Machine sales will greatly benefit the Linux OS.Steam OS is based on Arch Linux.The Steam Machine could deliver millions of new Linux users.Valve has finally given us the details for the Steam Machine. Hip, hip, huzzah!
