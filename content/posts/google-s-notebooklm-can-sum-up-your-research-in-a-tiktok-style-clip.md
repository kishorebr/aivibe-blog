<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Google's NotebookLM is adding a new way to catch up on your notes: TikTok-style AI videos. The new feature is rolling out to Google AI Ultra and Pro subscribers, allowing NotebookLM to generate 60-second vertical AI clips based on the sources you upload to the app.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The <a href="https://x.com/NotebookLM/status/2071987494799716626">example shared by Google</a> details Australia's unsuccessful war on emus, pairing paper cutout-style AI art of emus with narration. It adds to some of the other ways NotebookLM lets you interact with your research, including by generating <a href="https://www.theverge.com/2024/12/13/24318099/google-notebooklm-audio-overviews-talk-plus">AI podcasts</a>, <a href="https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/889475/notebooklm-can-now-summarize-research-in-cinematic-video-overviews">cinematic videos</a>, and visual explainers.</p>
