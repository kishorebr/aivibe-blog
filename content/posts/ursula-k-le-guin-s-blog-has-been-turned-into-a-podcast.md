<p>For those who will never tire of the words of Ursula K. Le Guin, a special treat is on the way. The esteemed late author's <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.ursulakleguin.com/blog" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1">blog</a>, which she started in 2010 at the age of 81, is being rereleased as a podcast, <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://inyoursparetime.libsyn.com/trailer" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1"><em>In Your Spare Time</em></a>. But, the podcast will include everything: essays, poems and "even the ones that are mostly cat pictures," according to the announcement. The first episode will be released April 8 on Apple Podcasts, Spotify and other platforms.&nbsp;</p><p> From Le Guin's official <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.instagram.com/p/DWo9ASbGZTJ/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&amp;igsh=MzRlODBiNWFlZA==" data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1">Instagram</a> account, which is managed by her estate:</p><blockquote><p>We always wanted to hear a version of the blog that includes every single post, even the ones that are mostly cat pictures. So for the next two years and change, we'll release an episode every Wednesday.
