<p>Amazon&#39;s upcoming <em>God of War</em> live-action TV adaptation has cast Ryan Hurst as its Kratos. Hurst is already sporting a very Kratos-style beard, so he&#39;s already got the right vibe going for him. He has past credits on familiar shows such as <em>Sons of Anarchy</em> and <em>The Walking Dead</em>. The upcoming Amazon series also isn&#39;t his first rodeo acting in this universe; Hurst voiced Thor in the video game <em>God of War Ragnarok</em>.
