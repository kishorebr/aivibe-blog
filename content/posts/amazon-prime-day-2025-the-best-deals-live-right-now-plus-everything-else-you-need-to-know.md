<h2 id="jump-link-how-does-prime-day-work">How does Prime Day work?</h2> 
<p>Prime subscribers can go to Amazon's site on Prime Day to see all of the exclusive deals they have access to. If you're not a Prime subscriber and you go to Amazon to shop on Prime Day, you'll likely see tons of items on sale but only "with Prime;" those discounts will not be available to those who are not active Prime subscribers.</p>
