<span id="end-legacy-contents"></span><h2 id="jump-link-what-to-expect-from-sony-afeela-at-ces">What to expect from Sony Afeela at CES</h2>
<p>What&#39;s now the Afeela 1 has been shown in various incarnations <a data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/transportation/evs/sony-opens-afeela-1-ev-reservations-at-ces-2025-011826937.html">since CES 2020</a>, where it was originally announced as Vision-S. But so many more important details were confirmed in the past couple of years, including the price, which starts at a staggering $89,900. However, the earlier impressions were less than impressive, and as of CES 2025, that thought remains the same. Engadget&#39;s automotive expert Tim Stevens said earlier this year that the EV &quot;<a data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/transportation/evs/sonys-afeela-1-feels-like-a-playstation-4-in-the-ps5-era-184620132.html">feels like a PlayStation 4 in the PS5 era</a>,&quot; and that &quot;the car lost what little interesting styling it had while sticking true to some specifications that sounded good five years ago.&quot; Ouch.</p>
<p>But the Afeela 1 won&#39;t be the only vehicle on display.
