<p>There may have been some extra incentive for the Trump administration to get the <a data-i13n="elm:context_link;elmt:doNotAffiliate;cpos:1;pos:1" class="no-affiliate-link" href="https://www.engadget.com/big-tech/tiktok-finalizes-deal-for-its-us-entity-010543484.html">TikTok US deal</a> done. According to a report from <a data-i13n="elm:context_link;elmt:doNotAffiliate;cpos:2;pos:1" class="no-affiliate-link" href="https://www.wsj.com/tech/tiktok-deal-fee-trump-administration-5aa31c9f"><em>The Wall Street Journal</em></a>, the Trump administration is set to receive a total of $10 billion in the deal that allowed TikTok to remain in the US. The new investors who acquired stakes in the US entity of TikTok already paid a $2.5 billion fee to the administration when the deal closed in January, but <em>WSJ</em>'s latest report noted that the group of investors would continue to make payments until the total hits $10 billion.</p>
<p>After a group of investors, which includes Oracle along with the <a data-i13n="elm:context_link;elmt:doNotAffiliate;cpos:3;pos:1" class="no-affiliate-link" href="https://www.engadget.com/ea-confirms-it-will-go-private-in-55-billion-acquisition-133841614.html">Silver Lake</a> and MGX investment firms, acquired stakes in the US-based TikTok entity called TikTok USDS Joint Venture, the <a data-i13n="elm:context_link;elmt:doNotAffiliate;cpos:4;pos:1" class="no-affiliate-link" href="https://www.wsj.com/business/deals/u-s-government-expected-to-get-multibillion-dollar-fee-in-tiktok-deal-685e3944"><em>WSJ</em></a> previously reported that the administration would receive a "multibillion-dollar fee" for its work on the deal. To better contextualize the recently-revealed $10 billion fee the Trump administration is receiving, the US entity of TikTok was valued at $14 billion by Vice President JD Vance.</p>
<span id="end-legacy-contents"></span><p>The Trump administration has previously involved itself in major deals with other US corporations. Last year, the administration invested <a data-i13n="elm:context_link;elmt:doNotAffiliate;cpos:5;pos:1" class="no-affiliate-link" href="https://www.engadget.com/big-tech/trumps-administration-may-look-to-buy-a-stake-in-intel-213234862.html">$8.9 billion</a> into Intel and received a nearly 9 percent equity stake. In terms of unprecedented windfalls, the Trump administration also received a <a data-i13n="elm:context_link;elmt:doNotAffiliate;cpos:6;pos:1" class="no-affiliate-link" href="https://www.npr.org/2025/05/21/nx-s1-5406420/trump-accepts-qatar-plane-air-force-one">Boeing 747-8</a> as a gift from the Qatari government in May.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/big-tech/trump-administration-will-reportedly-get-10-billion-for-brokering-the-tiktok-deal-180954979.html?src=rss
