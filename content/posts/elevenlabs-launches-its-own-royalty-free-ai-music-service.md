<p>The company has been quietly testing the platform for some time, with <em>WSJ</em> indicating it has given 20 of its customers access to the model and that they&#39;ve used it to make stuff for films, TV shows, video games and apps. ElevenLabs hasn&#39;t specified who the 20 customers are, likely because people <a data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/ai/pinterest-will-now-tell-you-when-youre-looking-at-ai-generated-content-163008812.html">get angry about AI slop</a>.</p>
<p>As for training, the company has inked deals with two digital rights agencies for smaller music labels called Merlin Network and Kobalt Music Group. ElevenLabs co-founder and CEO Mati Staniszewski says he&#39;s aiming to get major labels on board. He also says that &quot;the model is strictly created on data that we have access to.&quot; This is good news for the company, as other music-generation platforms like Suno and Udio have been sued for <a data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/record-labels-sue-ai-music-generators-for-massive-infringement-of-recorded-music-172915925.html">alleged use of copyrighted works</a>.</p>

<p>AI-generated music is having something of a moment right now. The &quot;band&quot; Velvet Sundown is completely made up and <a data-i13n="cpos:5;pos:1" href="https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2025/jul/14/an-ai-generated-band-got-1m-plays-on-spotify-now-music-insiders-say-listeners-should-be-warned?utm_source=firefox-newtab-en-us">managed to amass millions of listens</a> on Spotify. It remains to be seen how much of that interest was based on actual fandom or morbid curiosity.</p>
<p>ElevenLabs is primarily known for its voice-generation technology. It created a news app that reads stories to consumers with AI-generated voices <a data-i13n="cpos:6;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/you-can-now-get-ai-judy-garland-or-james-dean-to-read-you-the-news-160023595.html">based on celebrities</a> like Judy Garland and James Dean. One of its tools was <a data-i13n="cpos:7;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/elevenlabs-reportedly-banned-the-account-that-deepfaked-bidens-voice-with-its-ai-tools-083355975.html">used to emulate Joe Biden&#39;s voice</a> in robocalls urging voters not to participate in a primary. It&#39;s also been used to <a data-i13n="cpos:8;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/ai-voice-tool-deepfake-celebrity-audio-clips-094648743.html">create deepfakes for other celebrities</a>.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/ai/elevenlabs-launches-its-own-royalty-free-ai-music-service-183033630.html?src=rss
