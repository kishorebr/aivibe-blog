<p>Netflix&#39;s library of streamable party games is <a target="_blank" class="no-affiliate-link link" href="https://www.netflix.com/tudum/articles/overcooked-all-you-can-eat-game-news" data-i13n="elm:context_link;elmt:doNotAffiliate;cpos:1;pos:1">expanding today</a> with a custom version of <em>Overcooked! All You Can Eat</em>. Netflix launched <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/entertainment/streaming/netflix-is-bringing-party-games-to-tvs-123034128.html" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">its cloud gaming program</a> with games like <em>Lego Party </em>and <em>Tetris Time Warp</em>, but <em>Overcooked </em>feels a bit unique because it features a roster of Netflix-affiliated characters from <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/entertainment/streaming/kpop-demon-hunters-is-netflixs-most-watched-movie-of-all-time-215857627.html" data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1"><em>KPop Demon Hunters</em></a> and <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/entertainment/tv-movies/the-final-season-of-stranger-things-now-has-an-action-packed-trailer-151355646.html" data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1"><em>Stranger Things</em></a>.</p><p>For the uninitiated, <em>Overcooked</em> plays like a more manic version of <em>Diner Dash</em>, where teams attempt to prepare food together in increasingly elaborate kitchens filled with obstacles. The original version of <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/overcooked-remastered-ps5-xbox-series-x-162327675.html" data-i13n="cpos:5;pos:1"><em>Overcooked! All You Can Eat</em></a> was released in 2020, and includes DLC and stages from previous versions of the game. Netflix&#39;s version bundles in the same content, and &quot;10 Netflix celebrity chefs&quot; including &quot;Dustin, Eleven, Lucas, and the Demogorgon from <em>Stranger Things</em>,&quot; and &quot;half-dozen faces from <em>KPop Demon Hunters</em>,&quot; like &quot;Mira, Rumi, Zoey, Jinu, Derpy and Sussie.&quot; Like Netflix&#39;s other streaming games, playing <em>Overcooked</em> also requires you to use a connected smartphone as a controller.</p><p>Offering a growing library of streaming games is part of Netflix&#39;s new strategy under Alan Tascan, a former executive from Epic Games. Tascan took over as <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/netflix-hires-former-epic-games-exec-as-new-president-of-games-212614285.html" data-i13n="cpos:6;pos:1">Netflix&#39;s President of Games</a> in 2024, and appeared to start revamping the company&#39;s plans not long after, <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/netflix-scuttles-plans-to-add-six-previously-announced-games-to-its-service-192946233.html" data-i13n="cpos:7;pos:1">cancelling the release of several mobile games</a> and reportedly <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/netflix-has-closed-its-aaa-gaming-studio-133014946.html" data-i13n="cpos:8;pos:1">shutting down its AAA game studio</a>. Netflix is also continuing to adapt video games into content for its platform. For example, A24 is <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/entertainment/co-op-game-overcooked-may-become-a-competition-reality-tv-show-on-netflix-222406920.html" data-i13n="cpos:9;pos:1">reportedly developing a game show</a> based on <em>Overcooked </em>for the streaming service.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/gaming/netflixs-version-of-overcooked-lets-you-play-as-huntrx-212515187.html?src=rss
