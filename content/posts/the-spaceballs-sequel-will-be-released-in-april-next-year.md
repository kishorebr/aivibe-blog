<p>There&#39;s finally a release date for the <em>Spaceballs</em> sequel — but before you get too excited, it&#39;s a whole year away. As first reported by <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://deadline.com/2026/04/spaceballs-release-date-1236773242/" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1"><em>Deadline</em></a>, Amazon MGM Studios announced on Friday night that the upcoming <em>Spaceballs</em> movie will hit theaters on April 23, 2027, right around the 40th anniversary of the first film. Several members of the original cast will be reprising their roles, according to <em>Deadline</em>, including Mel Brooks, Rick Moranis, Bill Pullman, George Wynder and Daphne Zuniga.&nbsp;</p><div><blockquote class="twitter-tweet" style="width:608px;height:773px;width:640px;height:773px;"><p lang="en" dir="ltr">Spaceballs: The Release Date. April 23, 2027. <a href="https://t.co/5Xv0BKmf7C">pic.twitter.com/5Xv0BKmf7C</a></p>— Amazon MGM Studios (@AmazonMGMStudio) <a href="https://twitter.com/AmazonMGMStudio/status/2040245224451285286?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">April 4, 2026</a></blockquote>


</div><p> Whispers of a potential <em>Spaceballs 2</em> go back a couple of years, but <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://bsky.app/profile/officialmelbrooks.bsky.social/post/3lrgcy27tyk2e" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">Brooks</a> officially confirmed in an extremely on-brand announcement video last summer that the movie is actually happening. Following <em>Deadline</em>&#39;s latest report, Amazon MGM Studios posted a screenshot of the article on X, along with the words, &quot;Spaceballs: The Release Date. April 23, 2027.&quot; The movie is being directed by Josh Greenbaum and written by Josh Gad, Dan Hernandez and Benji Samit, according to <em>Deadline</em>. Along with the returning cast members, it will star Gad, Keke Palmer (!!), Lewis Pullman and Anthony Carrigan.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/entertainment/tv-movies/the-spaceballs-sequel-will-be-released-in-april-next-year-213543871.html?src=rss
