The show will premiere on the HBO Max streaming network this December 25. It&#39;s a rare case where a big-ticket project is coming out earlier than anticipated instead of later; the series wasn&#39;t expected to arrive until 2027.&nbsp;</p><p>HBO has already proved its bona fides with lush adaptations of fantastical stories several times over, and this trailer looks like more of the same. The <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://deadline.com/2024/06/harry-potter-showrunner-director-1235983341/" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1">team</a> behind the camera includes notable names who have worked on series such as <em>Succession</em>, <em>Game of Thrones</em>, <em>The Last of Us</em> and <em>Killing Eve</em>. Harry Potter creator J.K. Rowling, who alienated many fans after outing herself as transphobic, is also one of the show&#39;s executive producers. </p><p>Engadget&#39;s Jess Conditt has already <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/hogwarts-legacy-boycott-editorial-110018998.html" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">written eloquently</a> on the struggle of when and how to engage with the Harry Potter franchise while rejecting Rowling&#39;s worldview, although the author’s involvement at the top level may make this adaptation a harder sell to the disillusioned community than, say, the <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/hogwarts-legacy-review-170041713.html" data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1"><em>Hogwarts Legacy</em> video game</a> where Rowling was barely involved and the studios took a more proactive approach toward presenting many types of diversity, including gender expression. If you’re hyped for this particular show, seems you&#39;ll have a shorter time to wait for it.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/entertainment/tv-movies/hbo-maxs-harry-potter-series-premieres-this-christmas-202818238.html?src=rss
