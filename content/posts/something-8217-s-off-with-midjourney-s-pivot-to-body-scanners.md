<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The company <a href="https://www.midjourney.com/medical/blogpost">announced</a> a futuristic ultrasound scanner that would dunk users into a vat of water and, hopefully, produce "something as powerful as MRI" yet "as casual as a trip to the spa." Midjourney says the goal is to help people live longer, better, and healthier lives. CEO David Holz has <a href="https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/952011/midjourney-medical-ai-ultrasound-scan">suggested</a> the system could one day be better than MRI. Experts are skeptical.
