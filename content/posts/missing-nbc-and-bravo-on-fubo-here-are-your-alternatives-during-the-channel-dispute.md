<p>It feels like 2025 is the year of corporate <a data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/entertainment/youtube/disney-channels-are-back-on-youtube-tv-014930463.html">standoffs and stalemates</a> among TV providers, with the latest being a contract dispute between streaming provider Fubo and NBCUniversal which has led to a blackout of all the latter&#39;s networks on the service. Channels like NBC, USA Network, Telemundo, and Bravo went dark on Fubo at 5PM ET on November 21, 2025, and as of now, there&#39;s no projected date for their return.&nbsp;</p>
