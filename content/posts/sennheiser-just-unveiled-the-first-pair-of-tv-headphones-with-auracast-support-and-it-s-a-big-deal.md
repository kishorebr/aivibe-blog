    Sennheiser just unveiled the first pair of TV headphones with Auracast support, and it's a big deal
     
    The Sennheiser RS 275 TV Headphones feature high-end audio, hours of comfort, and endless connectivity via Auracast.
      Written by 
            Jada Jones, EditorEditor  Jan. 21, 2026 at 5:41 p.m. PT                           SennheiserFollow ZDNET: Add us as a preferred source on Google.ZDNET's key takeawaysSennheiser debuts the RS 275 TV Headphones bundle.The bundle includes the HDR 275 headphones and the BTA1 Auracast transmitter.Auracast enables lower latency and removes brand lock-in for private TV viewing at home.Sennheiser is expanding its TV headphones catalog with the HDR 275 over-ear headphones, designed for private TV-watching sessions. The HDR 275 aren't the company's first headphones made for watching TV, but they are the first to bundle with a transmitter fitted with a wireless superpower: Bluetooth Auracast.The Sennheiser RS 275 TV Headphones bundle includes the RS 275 headphones, a BTA1 digital transmitter, a 3.5mm audio cable, an Optical-to-3.5mm cable, one USB-C-to-USB-C cable, a USB-A-to-USB-C cable, and a headphones stand. Consumers can plug the BTA1 transmitter into laptops, game consoles, stereo systems, and more via a 3.5mm connection.
