<p class="has-drop-cap has-text-align-none">AI was everywhere at the GDC Festival of Gaming this year. Vendors at the event pitched generative AI tools for things like making <a href="https://www.artificial.agency/">AI-driven NPCs</a> and <a href="https://tesana.ai/">even entire games from a chat box</a>. On the show floor, I spent 10 minutes playing a demo of a pixel-art fantasy world generated by Tencent's AI tools. In a briefing with Razer, I watched an AI assistant for QA automatically log issues in a shooter game.
