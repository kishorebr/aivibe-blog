<p>Fans of miniature plastic soldiers, rejoice. Games Workshop has brought a host of older Warhammer and Warhammer 40K video games to <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.warhammer-community.com/en-gb/articles/ud4dbah1/warhammer-classics-brings-legendary-games-back-to-your-pc/" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1">Steam for the first time</a>, alongside a dozen games that haven&#39;t been available on Valve&#39;s storefront for a few years. The new to Steam releases consist of three games from the Warhammer fantasy range — <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280870/Warhammer_Shadow_of_the_Horned_Rat_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1"><em>Shadow of the Horned Rat</em></a>, <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280940/Warhammer_Mark_of_Chaos__Gold_Edition_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830" data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1"><em>Mark of Chaos – Gold Edition</em></a> and <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280860/Warhammer_Dark_Omen_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830" data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1"><em>Dark Omen</em></a> — and four from its sci-fi 40K universe — <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280890/Warhammer_40000_Chaos_Gate_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830" data-i13n="cpos:5;pos:1"><em>Chaos Gate</em></a>, <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280930/Warhammer_40000_Fire_Warrior_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830" data-i13n="cpos:6;pos:1"><em>Fire Warrior</em></a>, <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280920/Final_Liberation_Warhammer_Epic_40000_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830" data-i13n="cpos:7;pos:1"><em>Final Liberation</em></a> and <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/4280900/Warhammer_40000_Rites_of_War_Classic/?curator_clanid=34274830" data-i13n="cpos:8;pos:1"><em>Rites of War</em></a>.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><div><div style="left:0;width:100%;height:0;position:relative;padding-bottom:56.25%;"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/crVfMM3by_k?rel=0" style="top:0;left:0;width:100%;height:100%;position:absolute;border:0;" allowfullscreen scrolling="no" data-embed-domain="youtube.com" data-provider-name="youtube"></iframe></div></div><p>If you&#39;re a Warhammer fan of a certain age, some of these may be formative experiences for you. I know they are for me. I can&#39;t count how many hours I spent playing <em>Chaos Gate </em>when I first discovered 40K at the age of 10. Yes, it was an <em>XCOM </em>clone, but by that point I didn&#39;t know about the MicroProse original, and Space Marines were cool.&nbsp;</p><p>Years later and as a Tau collector at the time, I also loved <em>Fire Warrior, </em>even if it wasn&#39;t the most polished or deep first-person shooter. I haven&#39;t played the other five games included in today&#39;s announcement, but I&#39;ve heard <em>Warhammer: Shadow of the Horned Rat </em>and <em>Warhammer 40K: Rites of War </em>are pretty good if you&#39;re into the setting or, in the latter case, a fan of the Eldar.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><p>To celebrate the re-release of these old gems, Games Workshop is running a <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://store.steampowered.com/sale/warhammer-classics" data-i13n="cpos:9;pos:1">Classics sale on Steam</a>, with discounts on all 19 re-releases. Plus, you can get discounts on some more recent releases, including the excellent <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/boltgun--words-of-vengeance-is-warhammers-grimdark-answer-to-typing-of-the-dead-193515536.html" data-i13n="cpos:10;pos:1"><em>Dawn of War – Definitive Edition</em> and <em>Dawn of War 2 – Anniversary Edition</em></a><em>. </em>If you&#39;re new to the Warhammer 40K universe, and would rather avoid a plastic addiction, one of those would be my first port of call, along with the excellent <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/warhammer-40k-space-marine-2-is-unsurprisingly-getting-a-sequel-200055031.html" data-i13n="cpos:11;pos:1"><em>Space Marine 2</em></a>.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/gaming/pc/games-workshop-brings-seven-classic-warhammer-games-to-steam-for-the-first-time-185432304.html?src=rss
