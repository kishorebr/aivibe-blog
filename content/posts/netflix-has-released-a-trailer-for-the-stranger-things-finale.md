<p>Tomorrow&#39;s the big day, and I don&#39;t just mean New Year&#39;s Eve. The series finale of <em>Stranger Things</em> airs tomorrow, and Netflix has released a <a data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AfQ13jsLDms">trailer for the last episode</a>. We&#39;re given only flashes of the final episode, along with a voiceover of an emotional Jim Hopper telling Eleven he needs her to fight one last time.</p>
<p>It&#39;s just over 1 minute and 30 seconds long, with what appears to be only about 30 seconds of new footage. There isn&#39;t much to go on here, as most of the footage features emotional moments from seasons past. Netflix no doubt is hoping to keep much of the excitement under wraps until tomorrow&#39;s premiere.</p>

<p>One particularly ominous moment shows Hopper walking around a smoke-filled room with almost zero visibility, gun drawn, when we see what looks to be Vecna appear behind him. Dustin lets out a &quot;mother of God&quot; as the so-crazy-it-just-might-work scheme the group cooked up in Chapter Seven appears to be well underway.</p>
