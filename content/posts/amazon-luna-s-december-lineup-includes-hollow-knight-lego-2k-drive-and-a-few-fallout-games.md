<p>Amazon Luna’s December lineup of games has been announced, and there are plenty of early Christmas presents to sink your teeth into. For those unaware, Prime members automatically qualify for <a data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1" href="https://luna.amazon.com/"><ins>Luna</ins></a> standard, the entry-level tier of Amazon’s cloud gaming service, and each month the company also hands out a number of codes for PC games that permanently join in your library once redeemed.</p>
<p>Among those claim-and-keep-forever games this month are the underrated open-world Lego racing game, <a data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/lego-2k-drive-lets-you-build-your-dream-race-car-brick-by-brick-113038327.html"><em>Lego 2K Drive</em></a>, the very pretty Metroidvania, <em>Bō: Path of the Teal Lotus</em>, and a couple of retro Fallout games, just in time for the <a data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/entertainment/tv-movies/fallouts-new-season-two-trailer-is-filled-with-quick-nods-to-the-source-material-161533128.html">new season</a> of the Prime Video show. Here’s the full list of games for December. Most come in the form of codes you can redeem on GOG, but a couple are tied to the Epic Games Store or Legacy Gaming.</p>
<span id="end-legacy-contents"></span><ul>
<li><p><em>LEGO 2K Drive</em></p></li>
<li><p><em>Bō: Path of the Teal Lotus</em></p></li>
<li><p><em>Fallout: A Post Nuclear Role Playing Game</em></p></li>
<li><p><em>Fallout 2</em></p></li>
<li><p><em>Forgotten Realms: The Archives - Collection One</em></p></li>
<li><p><em>Gylt</em></p></li>
<li><p><em>Forgotten Realms: The Archives - Collection Two</em></p></li>
<li><p><em>Christmas Adventure: Candy Storm</em></p></li>
<li><p><em>Gunslugs 2</em></p></li>
<li><p><em>Ashworld</em></p></li>
<li><p><em>Forgotten Realms: The Archives - Collection Three</em></p></li>
<li><p><em>Dreamscaper</em></p></li>
</ul>
<p>On the cloud side of things, Luna members can stream the likes of <em>Fallout 4: Game of the Year Edition</em>, with <em>Fallout 3 </em>and fan favorite <em>New Vegas </em><a data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/prime-gamings-october-2025-lineup-will-get-you-in-the-mood-for-fallout-season-2-170052175.html"><ins>already</ins></a> available. Quite the month for wasteland wanderers, then. <em>Mafia: Definitive Edition</em>, <em>Hollow Knight </em>and <em>Shadow of the Tomb Raider: Definitive Edition </em>also feature in the current library.</p>
