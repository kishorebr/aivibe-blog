<p>The hit mountain climbing simulation <em>Cairn</em> is getting a series of free DLC drops, under the banner <em>On the Trail</em>. The first will be released this summer and <a data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sQxGyHqPrxk"><ins>it&#39;s called </ins><em><ins>Deep Water</ins></em></a>.</p>
<p>The DLC introduces three new climbing areas and plenty of &quot;fresh challenges.&quot; There&#39;s also a new avatar, as players can now climb as Aava or the game&#39;s original sidekick Marco.</p>
<span id="end-legacy-contents"></span><div id="e855b06771ef423dac516ae78f4133f8"><iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/sQxGyHqPrxk?si=x21yM_i3f5_Djw8H" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></div>
<p>As the name suggests, there&#39;s some new water-based mechanics here. It looks like players will be able to fall into the water without automatically dying, though the developer hasn&#39;t shown how that works exactly. In the original game, a fall into a body of water would be treated just like any other fall.</p>
<p>This is the first in a series of new DLC releases, but we don&#39;t know what the other updates will entail. We do know that they&#39;ll all be free. The base game is $30, when not on sale, so this is a nice little perk for fans.</p>
<div id="5254e0bf337149caa949bfbfd384fdfd"><iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/zkWA6ASFoj4?si=Qng4VIcVVqQDX7uK" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></div>
<p>For the uninitiated, <em>Cairn</em> is a <a data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/the-game-bakers-cairn-turns-mountain-climbing-into-a-boss-rush-220638117.html"><ins>serious mountain climbing sim</ins></a> that forces players to keep a keen eye on balance and stamina. It&#39;s got a fairly complex control scheme, with a manual mode that mandates independent control of each limb. There&#39;s an emphasis on route planning and gear management. The game is also quite easy on the eyes.</p>
<p>Today&#39;s DLC announcement came as part of the <a data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/how-to-watch-the-triple-i-initiative-showcase-on-april-9-170353957.html"><ins>Triple-i Initiative showcase</ins></a>, which spotlights indie games. The stream also gave us reveals for a new Don&#39;t Starve game and an intriguing title from the devs <a data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/1000xresist-devs-reveal-their-wild-looking-second-game-about-convincing-an-ai-its-not-human-170018986.html"><ins>behind the hit narrative adventure </ins><em><ins>1000xResist</ins></em></a>.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/gaming/mountain-climbing-sim-cairn-is-getting-free-dlc-this-summer-182908992.html?src=rss
