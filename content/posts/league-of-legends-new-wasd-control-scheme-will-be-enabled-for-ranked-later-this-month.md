<p>Riot Games released a pile of updates for its long-running MOBA <em>League of Legends</em>. One of the more noteworthy changes coming to the game is the official launch of WASD controls. This alternate option, allowing players to traverse the rift by keyboard rather than by mouse, is rolling out to ranked matches in patch 26.9.&nbsp;</p><p>Riot first <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/pc/riot-is-trying-to-make-lol-more-accessible-by-adding-wasd-controls-170549596.html" data-i13n="cpos:1;pos:1">announced</a> that it was pursuing support for WASD controls last August. The studio said it wanted to ensure that the alternate control scheme wasn&#39;t more powerful than point-and-click movement; Riot said it was targeting a low win-loss rate difference between the options before releasing it to <em>League</em> players. &quot;There&#39;s still a small delta in the win-rates between the control schemes, with Point and Click having a minor advantage,&quot; according to today&#39;s <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.leagueoflegends.com/en-us/news/dev/dev-wasds-ranked-release/" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">devlog</a> dedicated to this new feature. &quot;We expect that difference will decrease over time as players gain more mastery with WASD, but we will continue to monitor this stat in the future.&quot;&nbsp;</p><div><div style="left:0;width:100%;height:0;position:relative;padding-bottom:56.25%;"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/K1TLR8HQgiU?rel=0" style="top:0;left:0;width:100%;height:100%;position:absolute;border:0;" allowfullscreen scrolling="no" data-embed-domain="youtube.com" data-provider-name="youtube"></iframe></div></div><p>That blog post goes into more detail about how the team tested and gauged community responses to WASD, which is pretty neat stuff if you&#39;re a game dev nerd. <em>League</em> will be receiving a few new accessibility improvements, such as custom inputs for moving the mouse cursor and some new flexibility for keybinds, along with the new control scheme. Although Riot was clear to say that it&#39;s not adding official support for controllers or gamepads, players will be able to use WASD controls with a joystick.</p>This article originally appeared on Engadget at https://www.engadget.com/gaming/league-of-legends-new-wasd-control-scheme-will-be-enabled-for-ranked-later-this-month-193858052.html?src=rss
