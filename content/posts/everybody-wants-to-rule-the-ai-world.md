<p class="has-text-align-none">Sometimes, companies pick CEOs based on carefully laid succession plans designed to maximize investor confidence and future performance. Such was the story of The Blip, the days in 2024 when Sam Altman was ousted from OpenAI. We knew that situation was chaotic; <a href="https://www.theverge.com/tech/917225/sam-altman-elon-musk-openai-lawsuit">the ongoing Musk v. Altman trial</a> is showing just how chaotic it really was.</p>
