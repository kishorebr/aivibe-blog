The game&#39;s publisher Fireshine Games announced its launch date alongside <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TRWwQY199Yk" data-i13n="cpos:2;pos:1">a new trailer</a> at Nintendo&#39;s recent <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.engadget.com/gaming/nintendo/nintendo-just-announced-an-indie-world-showcase-for-tomorrow-at-9am-et-164706666.html" data-i13n="cpos:3;pos:1">Indie World event</a>.</p><p>If it wasn&#39;t clear from previous trailers, <em>Denshattack!</em> remains stylish as hell, filled to the brim with bright colors and over-the-top animation. The new trailer doesn&#39;t offer much more in terms of what the story of the game will be, but it does highlight its fast-paced gameplay, which will have you flipping and grinding a tiny train through settings like a futuristic cityscape, jungles and a snowy mountain region.</p><p>Among the other charms of <em>Denshattack!</em>, the game also features original songs from composers Tee Lopes (<em>Sonic Mania</em>), Ryo Nagamatsu (Splatoon series), Richard Jacques (Jet Set Radio series) and Takenobu Mitsuyoshi (<em>Daytona USA</em>). If you&#39;re at all curious to try <em>Denshattack!</em> before it comes out this summer, demos of the game are available to download right now for <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/2524850/Denshattack/" data-i13n="cpos:4;pos:1">PC</a> and <a target="_blank" class="link" href="https://www.nintendo.com/us/store/products/denshattack-switch-2/" data-i13n="cpos:5;pos:1">Switch 2</a>.</p><p><em>Denshattack! </em>will be available on PC, PlayStation 5, Switch 2 and Xbox Series X/S when it launches June 17.
